About

Welcome to my interactive 3D globe experience. This project showcases a stylized representation of Earth with real-time location tracking and time display.

The globe features a monochromatic design with a focus on the landmasses, surrounded by a subtle atmospheric glow against a starry background.

Built using Three.js, this interactive visualization detects your current location and displays it on the globe with a small green marker.
